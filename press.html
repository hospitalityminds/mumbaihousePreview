Announcement: Bangkok to Sihanoukville flight route

06 June 2019

Song Saa Private Island is delighted to welcome a new access route to our calm shores.

From July 1st, Sihanoukville International Airport (KOS) will receive a four-time weekly flight from Bangkok Don Mueang International Airport (DMK). A short flight time of only one hour means a quick and easy access from one of Asia’s most vibrant hubs to the sandy coast of Cambodia. Air Asia Join JC International Airways in flying this route, with the latter leaving from Suvarnabhumi International Airport (BKK).
