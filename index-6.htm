Affecting Change in the World in a Sustainable Way

Four Ways to Be the Good

Donate

Making a one-time donation helps to fund sustainable community education, training for local collaborators, and treatment for patients in need.

Become a Monthly Contributor

Contributing to the cause each month ensures the sustainability and vitality of efforts put forth to provide education, training, and treatment for communities in great need.

Volunteer

Donate your time and expertise to the cause. With opportunities to serve globally and domestically, help save lives through research and innovation, warehouse efforts, or start a friend-raiser.

Become a Strategic Partner

Partnerships with businesses and organizations are needed to leverage capacity-building efforts and humanitarian aid. This approach leads to greater impact in medical and health education, training, and treatment goals.

Help Us Save Lives & Bring Hope to the World

International Medical Relief provides sustainable capacity-building and direct relief efforts. Everyone plays a vital role in saving and changing lives. Our international medical corps of volunteers provides long-term comprehensive large and small scale clinics offering life-saving interventions and high impact training to vulnerable communities around the globe. According to the World Health Organization, approximately 5.3 million children die of preventable diseases each year that could be saved through access to focused, scaleable education, treatment, and diagnoses. With careful integration of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, our work create a blueprint for communities to reach their greatest level of success. We invite you to be the good.
