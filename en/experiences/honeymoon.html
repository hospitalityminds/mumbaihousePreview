IS CAMBODIA A GOOD HONEYMOON DESTINATION?

Cambodia is the perfect place for newlyweds to enjoy pristine beaches wrapped in ancient culture and tradition. This surprisingly picturesque Southeast Asian country is known for its cheerful local people and incredible food. The islands and beaches on Cambodia’s west coast are nothing short of paradise, with their pure white sand and clear turquoise waters. Find out more about the ideal Cambodian honeymoon at Song Saa Private Island.

WHY IS SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND THE BEST RESORTING CAMBODIA FOR YOUR HONEYMOON?

Song Saa Private Island is the ultimate luxury resort experience for newlyweds. If you want your honeymoon to be filled with sensual pampering experiences and a grounding closeness to the majesty of nature, then Song Saa Private Island is the venue you are looking for. Song Saa Private Island offers unparalleled privacy, creating an intimate and romantic atmosphere for you and your partner. The level of service and attention to detail provided by the Song Saa Private Island hosts (most of whom are locals) is exceptional.

IS SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND AN ETHICAL HONEYMOON CHOICE?

Song Saa Private Island is a leading retreat for rest, romance and relaxation, but it is also an ethical accommodation choice. Song Saa Private Island prides themselves on being the first ecotourism destination in Cambodia. Song Saa Private Island is dedicated to protecting and preserving the beautiful island archipelago that they are honored to be a part of. The opulent resort design was inspired by the splendid natural environment and built by locals with locally reclaimed and salvaged materials. Sustainability and harmony with the environment underpin everything at Song Saa Private Island. Between the twin islands is Cambodia’s first Marine Reserve, established to protect the area’s diverse marine flora and fauna. The Song Saa Private Island Foundation is committed to the conservation of the natural environment and supporting local community programs. Find out more about the foundation’s invaluable work .

IS SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND A PRIVATE PLACE FOR AN INTIMATE HONEYMOON?

Song Saa Private Island is a supremely private setting for you to spend precious time with your loved one. Escape the rest of the world at this tranquil haven. The island is only accessible via boat or helicopter, and for your privacy, the use of personal drones is prohibited on the island. Song Saa Private Island’s peaceful over water villas are nestled into a private portion of the shoreline and feature a private infinity pool, balcony and views.For a completely secluded, off-the-grid experience check out the Royal Villa .

WHAT ROMANTIC EXPERIENCES DOES SONG SEMIPRIVATE ISLAND OFFER FOR NEWLYWEDS?

There is a wide range of enchanting and memorable experiences that couples can choose from while staying at Song Saa Private Island.The honeymoon packages include petals on the bed, a flower bath, and special arrival amenities, as well as a sensual couple’s massage, Khmer Monk blessing ceremony and private destination dining experience. Once you are completely rejuvenated, why not try an exploration of the surrounding islands on the magical sunset cruise. Couples can also indulge in a captivating snorkeling experience, viewing the unusual and plentiful sea life on the island's reefs. Alternatively, take a peaceful walk through the untouched tropical jungle of the ancient volcanic islands. Song Saa Private Island also offers many other sensational activities for both the adventurous or the serenity-seeking traveler.
