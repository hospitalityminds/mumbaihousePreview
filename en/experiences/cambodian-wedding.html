Private Island For Cambodian Wedding Venues

At Song Saa
The two discreet islands that make up our resort are known locally as Song Saa – Khmer for The Sweethearts –and they are as romantic as the name implies.

Song Saa Private Island offers uncompromising luxury set in a spectacular tropical seascape with alabaster beaches, rainforests, and locally sourced fresh cuisine – a perfect venue for an exclusive and intimate wedding.

Couples can choose from any number of magnificent and private locations on Song Saa’s twin islands to exchange their vows and create romantic memories to last a life time.

And when the ceremony is over, Song Saa Private Island’s exquisite overwater restaurant and lounge is a stunning setting to raise a glass of Champagne in celebration and indulge in an evening of inspiring dining.

Song Saa offers tailored wedding concepts for couples who like to do things in impeccable, unforgettable style. It’s impossible to imagine a more romantic start to your life together.

BEACHFRONT 


This is the most photogenic and stunning location for the ceremony. This venue provides a scenic backdrop benefiting from a stunning sunset panorama directly behind the ceremonial gazebo.

The vista and ambience combined with the smiling faces of your nearest and dearest assure a magical experience.


VISTA OVERWATER RESTAURANT & LOUNGE

 Sitting just off the island’s shoreline, surrounded by our house reef and gazetted marine reserve, this incredible space seats an intimate 36 in the main venue as well as 12 on the open air deck. For events, undercover seating for 48 with an additional 16 on the open-air deck.

DRIFTWOOD BAR & KITCHEN


Located on the beach, Driftwood has a relaxed and rustic feel, offering a pizza and tapas-style menu and is the perfect spot for a cold beer, a chilled glass of wine or one of our signature cocktails.

BESPOKE & PRIVATISATION


Why not create the wedding of your dreams... Enjoy a private island in the purest of sense.

With multiple possible configurations for ceremonial venues, catering and mixers, organise an Island home for your nearest and dearest and no-one else. Have a personalised service before, during and after your big
day.

Island rental is the most highly recommended option for a guaranteed private, intimate and personalised experience. Connect with us to find out more.




DOES SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND, CAMBODIA OFFER A WEDDING PACKAGE?

Yes! Song Saa Private Island, Cambodia is the perfect location for an exclusive destination wedding. Song Saa Private Island offers bespoke wedding packages designed to fulfill all your wedding dreams. The wedding package includes a dreamy island ceremony, gourmet dinner for you and your guests, a make-up assistant, fresh tropical flowers and much more. If you can imagine it – Song Saa Private Island’s professional wedding planner can make it happen!

WHY SHOULD YOU CHOOSE SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND FOR YOUR WEDDING?

If you are looking for a breathtakingly beautiful, romantic wedding venue, then Song Saa Private Island is the place for you. The two charming islets that make up the private island resort were given the name ‘Song Saa’ by the locals. This translates into ‘The Sweethearts’. A ceremony at this luxurious, 5-star resort will undoubtedly be an idyllic and unforgettable start to your lives together. The Vista Over water Lounge and Restaurant is an incredible reception venue with unbelievable views and a mouth-watering menu featuring an exotic fusion of contemporary Western and traditional Cambodian cuisine

CAN YOU HAVE A BEACH WEDDING AT SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND?

Absolutely! Song Saa Private Island offers a range of exquisite wedding locations, but beachfront weddings are the most popular. Imagine your wedding, on a magnificent beach at sunset, with the warm sand under your feet, and the soothing sound of the water lapping at the shore, all framed by a lavish ceremonial gazebo. The beach wedding package also includes shaded pool-side cocktails or dinner after the seaside ceremony. Round off your beach wedding by staying in a gorgeous over-water villa, with portholes so you can watch the crystalline waters and exotic marine life.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN A WEDDING AT SONGSAA PRIVATE ISLAND?

The best thing about a Song Saa Private Island wedding is that it is completely customization to meet the needs of you and your other half. The Song Saa Private Island wedding planner will help you to design the wedding ceremony and reception of your dreams. You have the choice of a range of ceremonial venues, reception food and accommodation for your nearest and dearest. Because this is your special day, the SongSaa Private Island team want you to be completely happy with your bespoke wedding experience. The wedding planner will take care of the wedding décor, flowers, cake and other aspects so that you don’t have to worry about a thing when you arrive in paradise. Island rental is recommended for a guaranteed private, intimate and personalized experience. Discover our exclusive packages in our wedding brochure.

ARE PROFESSIONAL PHOTOS/VIDEO INCLUDED IN A SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND WEDDING?

An intimate ceremony at the Utopian SongSaa Private Island is something you will want to remember forever. Song Saa Private Island works with skilled photographers and video-graphers to make your special moment last a lifetime. Just remember to let the wedding planner know you would like a photographer and/or videographer at your nuptials. Take a peep at the stunning pictures in the wedding gallery to get an idea of how your special day can look.

WHAT IS A KHMER MONK BLESSING CEREMONY AT SONG SAA PRIVATE ISLAND?

This ceremony, performed by traditional Cambodian Buddhist Monks, is the perfect way to incorporate the local culture and wisdom into your unique island experience. Hosts from Song Saa Private Island will explain the ceremony process and its deep cultural significance before your blessing. During the ceremony, the Monks will best upon your union an ancient and sacred water blessing. You and your partner will both receive a red string bracelet dipped in blessed water as a physical reminder of your spiritual experience.
