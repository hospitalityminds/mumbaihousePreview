Message from our founder

- Melita Koulmandas Hunter



Soursuday, to everyone in our global community from the islands of Cambodia.

We hope you and those around you are safe and well. The Song Saa Family are all safe here in the Koh Rong Archipelago and here to help sort the best travel solution for you of Cambodia.

We firmly believe that we have a role to play in the collective effort to reduce the transmission and effect of COVID-19. We are closely monitoring and implementing all World Health Organisation and Centers for Disease Control and Prevention guidelines, as well as working with authorities and the wider travel industry to lead the way in implementing best practices during this time. 

Our goal is to ensure that all people are safe, including our wider community - our staff, locals, and you. We endeavour to make every experience you have with Song Saa one that helps keep travel restorative. During this time that means ensuring that changing your travel plans is as comfortable and painless as possible.


Support During This Time


We would ask you to bear in mind that this is an incredibly tough time for the entire travel industry. If you are looking for ways you can support us, the best way to help is by making a new booking or postponing an existing booking. This allows us to continue the amazing work our team has been doing in the wider community through sustainability, education, and other outreach programs - something that is more important than ever right now.

We have a range of offers at the moment for both bookings in the immediate future and more long-term.

It also means that we can provide you with what we do best -- a rejuvenating stay. The entire team feels a new sense of importance in leading the way in how we travel and experience places. This is something we would love you to join us in.

Ultimately, though, we will support you to do whatever you feel is best for you and your loved ones.


For Existing Bookings


All existing bookings made can be postponed or cancelled for stays until 20 December 2021. This change or cancellation will be made without a fee until 30 September 2020.

Please don’t hesitate to contact us on [email protected] if you need help with your travel plans, rescheduling your trip or have any other concerns. Throughout this difficult time, we would like to thank you all for your continued support and send wishes of good health.


New Bookings


We are taking bookings now for stays from now until 30 September 2020. We understand that it is a confusing time to travel. We are addressing this with:

  • Full refund or amendment to any booking made between now and 30 September 2020.
  • Comprehensive health and safety guidelines, including temperature checking of all staff and guests travelling, and industry best practice sanitation techniques.
Please see the below FAQ section for more details. 

The Song Saa family wish you a safe and successful continuation, 

Sincerely, 
Melita and the entire Song Saa Family

Frequently Asked Questions


  • What is the Resort’s cancellation policy?  
If you cancel your booking which required a deposit or payment in advance, we will do everything we can to process your refund as quickly as possible. 

  • Can I reschedule my reservation for a later date?
Yes. All existing bookings and those made until 20 December 2020 may be changed for stays between now and 20 December 2021

  • I booked through a third party vendor, can you help me?
For bookings made through an online booking platform or third party travel professionals, please contact them directly. We have advised these companies of our cancellation policy and you should refer to their terms and conditions.

  • How do I contact guest reservations?
If you have a specific query regarding your booking you may contact our Reservations team at [email protected]

  • What is the hotel doing right now for health and safety?

  1. On property, all our employees have completed a professional hygiene and health well-being training by our Resort Doctor.
  2. All touch points in our villas and public spaces are thoroughly sanitised twice a day.  All private pool water is drained and replaced after every guest check-out.
  3. Pool water is recycled for use in our gardens. 
  4. Prior to boarding our Song Saa Private Island speedboat in Sihanoukville Port, your temperature will be taken to ensure that you don’t have a fever, the same policy applies to anyone visiting the Island.
  5. Our boat is sanitised before and after each journey to the mainland. 
  6. Hand sanitiser is offered to all guests upon boarding. 
  7. Every guest of Song Saa Private Island is required by Cambodian law to complete a medical waiver and questionnaire about your travel schedule as part of the reservation process or prior to boarding our speed boat to the Island. 
  8. Your temperature is monitored daily by our Resort Doctor throughout your stay with us.
  9. Please note that reservations will only be confirmed after showing valid proof of having been in the country for 14 or more days prior to arrival date. We reserve the right to reject or cancel a booking if guests don’t meet this requirement. 
  10. Work spaces, offices and back of house areas are sanitised on a daily basis. 
  11. The resort uses FORWARD, a disinfecting chemical from Diversey containing ethoxylated alcohol and ammonium chloride which does not deteriorate hard surfaces.
  12. Face masks are available with our Guest Experience team on request and subject to availability.

  • What is hotel doing to protect employees?
Our Resort Doctor conducted a mandatory hygiene and health well-being training attended by all our employees. Those who have travelled during this time are monitored with daily temperature checks by our doctor and must wear a face mask while interacting with guests and colleagues for a minimum of 14 days. Staff  practices social distancing in our canteen and also during internal meetings. Temperature checks are conducted daily before the start of duty for all employees.  We are moving towards contactless service where possible by our Song Saa family members who are guest facing, such as in-villa dining and in our restaurants. The resort is also providing Contactless service through our In-Villa Dining team when you order Breakfast, Lunch or Dinner in your Villa.

To date (14 September 2020) Song Saa Private Island has no cases of Coronavirus (Covid-19) affecting our operations. We continue to monitor the evolving situation and will be keeping this policy under review.
