Changing Lives

Find out more

$25 Monthly

You will guarantee that a vulnerable child receives: 

  • A Hygiene Pack including one year’s supply of toothpaste, brushes, soap and washcloths; 
  • Education on the best dental care and hygiene practices to live a healthy and happy life, with a big smile on their face!

$50 Monthly

  • Enable employment of two women in the community to execute the recycle program; 
  • Establishment of a recycling centre for plastic bottles and aluminium cans
  • Education and promotion of reusing plastic materials through arts and crafts, which in turn supports alternate development; and removing over the targeted volume of 2 tons of waste in the Koh Rong Archipelago.

$100 Monthly

You will assist Song Saa Foundation in purchasing a hydrologic water filter for a family, which eliminates 99.9% of bacteria and viruses, greatly reducing water-borne illnesses and giving them clean drinking water for up to and over two years and building 20,000 Litre water catchment structures in 4 villages of the Koh Rong Archipelago.
